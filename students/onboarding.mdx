## Welcome to Kairo

Kairo is an AI-powered learning platform designed to help you master full-stack development through personalized learning paths. Your journey begins with a comprehensive onboarding process that adapts to your unique learning style.

## Onboarding Process

The onboarding assessment consists of 30 questions across 7 categories designed to understand how you learn best.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete the Learning Style Assessment">
    Answer 30 questions that evaluate:
    - **Sensory Channels (VARK)** - Visual, Auditory, Reading/Writing, Kinesthetic preferences
    - **Information Preferences** - How you prefer to receive and process information
    - **Processing & Perception** - Active vs. Reflective learning styles
    - **Study Habits** - Your approach to studying and problem-solving
    - **Learning Environment** - Your ideal conditions for learning
    - **Experiential Cycle** - How you approach new challenges
    - **Attitude Toward Learning** - Your motivation and learning mindset
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review Your Learning Profile">
    After completing the assessment, you'll receive:
    - Your primary learning style (Visual, Kinesthetic, Reading, Auditory, or Mixed)
    - Soft skills baseline scores (1-5 scale) for:
      - Autonomy
      - Time Management
      - Problem Solving
      - Communication
      - Teamwork
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Assigned to Your Clan">
    You'll be placed in a learning clan where you'll collaborate with peers and receive guidance from your Team Leader (TL).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Your Learning Journey">
    Access your personalized dashboard with tailored content, assignments, and AI-generated learning plans.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Learning Style Categories

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Visual Learners" icon="eye">
    Learn best with diagrams, charts, videos, and visual elements. Your dashboard will emphasize visual resources and concept maps.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Kinesthetic Learners" icon="hand">
    Learn best by doing - building projects and practicing with real code. You'll get more hands-on exercises and coding challenges.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Reading/Writing Learners" icon="book">
    Learn best through documentation, written notes, and text-based materials. Expect detailed written guides and summaries.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Auditory Learners" icon="headphones">
    Learn best through listening and verbal explanations. Your content will include more audio resources and discussion opportunities.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Your Soft Skills Profile

The onboarding assessment establishes your baseline in five critical soft skills:

| Skill | Description | How It's Used |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **Autonomy** | Your ability to work independently and take initiative | Influences the level of guidance in your learning plans |
| **Time Management** | How effectively you organize and prioritize tasks | Affects deadline recommendations and pacing |
| **Problem Solving** | Your approach to tackling challenges | Determines complexity of exercises and hints provided |
| **Communication** | How well you express ideas and collaborate | Impacts group project assignments |
| **Teamwork** | Your ability to work effectively in teams | Influences collaborative activity frequency |

<Note>
Your soft skills scores are not final grades - they're starting points. Kairo's AI continuously tracks your progress and adapts to your growth.
</Note>

## What Happens Next?

Once onboarding is complete:

1. **Dashboard Access** - View your personalized learning dashboard with current module, weekly progress, and performance metrics
2. **Module Assignment** - Get assigned to your first technical module (e.g., Backend Fundamentals, Frontend Development)
3. **Learning Plan Generation** - Receive an AI-generated 20-day complementary plan targeting your weakest soft skill
4. **Team Leader Connection** - Your TL can view your profile and provide personalized feedback

<Tip>
Be honest in your responses! The assessment is designed to help you, not test you. There are no "right" answers - only insights into your learning preferences.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I retake the onboarding assessment?">
    The initial onboarding is completed once. However, your learning profile updates automatically as you progress through modules and activities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my learning style changes?">
    Your learning preferences may evolve over time. Kairo's AI adapts to your actual behavior and performance, not just your initial assessment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does onboarding take?">
    The 30-question assessment typically takes 10-15 minutes. Take your time and answer thoughtfully.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the assessment available in English?">
    Yes! The assessment supports both Spanish (ES) and English (EN). Toggle the language in the top-right corner.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
