## Overview

Your student profile is your professional identity within Kairo. It tracks your learning progress, displays your skills, and can be exported as a polished CV for job applications.

## Accessing Your Profile

Click your avatar or name in the top-right corner, then select **"Mi Perfil"** from the dropdown menu.

## Profile Sections

### 1. Profile Header



Displays:
- **Profile Photo:** Upload a custom avatar (supports JPG, PNG, up to 5MB)
- **Full Name:** Your name from registration
- **Role Badge:** "Coder" designation
- **Quick Stats:**
  - Average Score: Your Moodle performance average
  - Current Week: Week number in your module

### 2. Personal Information

**Read-Only Fields:**
- **Email:** Your registered email address (cannot be changed)

**Editable Fields:**
- **Phone Number:** Optional contact number
- **Location:** City and country (e.g., "Medellín, Colombia")
- **Birth Date:** Your date of birth (optional, used for age-appropriate content)
- **Bio:** A short description about yourself (200 characters max)

<Note>
Personal information is private and only visible to you and your Team Leader.
</Note>

## Editing Your Profile

### Edit Mode

Click the **Edit Profile** button (pen icon) to enter edit mode:

**What Changes:**
- Input fields replace read-only text for editable fields
- Skill pills show remove buttons (X)
- "Add Skill" input appears
- **Save Changes** button becomes visible
- **Preview CV** and **Download CV** buttons hide temporarily

**Saving:**

```javascript
POST /api/profile/update
{
  "personalInfo": {
    "phone": "+57 300 123 4567",
    "location": "Medellín, Colombia",
    "birthDate": "2000-05-15",
    "bio": "Full-stack developer passionate about building scalable web apps."
  },
  "socials": {
    "github": "github.com/johndoe",
    "linkedin": "linkedin.com/in/johndoe",
    "twitter": "twitter.com/johndoe",
    "portfolio": "johndoe.dev"
  },
  "metadata": {
    "skills": ["JavaScript", "React", "Node.js", "PostgreSQL"]
  }
}
```

**Success:**
- Green toast notification: **"Perfil actualizado correctamente"**
- Edit mode exits automatically
- Profile re-renders with updated data

**Error Handling:**
- Red toast notification with error message
- Edit mode remains active so you can fix issues
- Common errors: Empty required fields, invalid date format

### Canceling Edits

Click the **X** button (same Edit Profile button, now showing an X) to exit edit mode without saving. Changes are discarded.

## Avatar Upload

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hover Over Avatar">
    Hover over your profile photo to reveal the edit overlay.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Cambiar foto'">
    Click the overlay to open the file picker.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Image">
    Choose a JPG or PNG file (max 5MB recommended).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Auto-Save">
    The image uploads immediately and updates across all views (dashboard, profile, topbar).
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Technical Details:**
- Images are converted to Base64 and stored in MongoDB `profile_metadata`
- The `avatarService` caches your avatar to reduce load times
- Calling `invalidateMyAvatar()` after upload clears the cache

<Warning>
Large images (>2MB) may take a few seconds to upload. Wait for the success toast before navigating away.
</Warning>

## CV Generation

Kairo automatically generates a professional CV from your profile data.

### Preview CV

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Preview CV'">
    Click the **Preview CV** button in the top-right of your profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Modal">
    A modal opens showing your formatted CV with:
    - Header with your name and contact info
    - Professional summary (your bio)
    - Technical skills as colorful badges
    - Social links section
  </Step>

  <Step title="Download (Optional)">
    Click **Download PDF** in the modal to export.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Read-Only Mode (Team Leader View)

When your Team Leader views your profile:
- They see all your information in **read-only mode**
- Edit buttons are hidden
- CV preview/download buttons are hidden (TLs can't export your CV)
- The page title changes to **"Perfil de [Your Name]"**
- A back button navigates to the TL dashboard

<Note>
This allows TLs to review your progress and provide personalized feedback without modifying your data.
</Note>

## Data Privacy

Your profile data is stored securely:

**PostgreSQL (users table):**
- Email, full name, role, clan
- Current module and progress

**PostgreSQL (user_profiles table):**
- Personal info (phone, location, birth date)
- Bio text
- Social links (GitHub, LinkedIn, etc.)

**MongoDB (profile_metadata):**
- Avatar URL (Base64 image)
- Skills array
- Custom metadata

**Who Can See Your Profile:**
- ✅ You (full edit access)
- ✅ Your Team Leader (read-only)
- ❌ Other students (cannot view)
- ❌ Students from other clans (cannot view)

## Troubleshooting

### Avatar Not Updating

**Problem:** Avatar shows old image after upload.  
**Solution:** Hard refresh (`Ctrl+Shift+R` or `Cmd+Shift+R`) to clear browser cache.

### "Failed to Save" Error

**Problem:** Changes don't persist when clicking Save.  
**Solutions:**
- Check that required fields aren't empty
- Ensure birth date is in `YYYY-MM-DD` format
- Verify your session hasn't expired (try logging out and back in)

### CV Download Not Working

**Problem:** PDF doesn't download when clicking button.  
**Solutions:**
- Ensure you have a bio and at least one skill added
- Check browser's pop-up blocker settings
- Try the "Preview CV" modal instead and download from there

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my email address?">
    No. Your email is tied to your account and cannot be changed. Contact your TL or an administrator if you need to update it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my profile public?">
    No. Only you and your Team Leader can view your profile. It's not visible to other students or the public internet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I delete my avatar?">
    Currently, you can only replace your avatar with a new image. If you want to remove it entirely, contact support.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How often should I update my profile?">
    Update your profile whenever you:
    - Learn a new technology (add to skills)
    - Complete a major project (update bio)
    - Create new social profiles (add links)
    - Change contact information
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my CV be used for job applications?">
    Absolutely! The generated CV is professional-quality and suitable for early-career developer positions. Customize your bio and skills to match each job posting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
